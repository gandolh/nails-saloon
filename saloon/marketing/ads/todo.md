# Ad Strategy — Ana Saloon (Târgu-Jiu)

Plan-only. Goal: a steady flow of **bookings** (WhatsApp programări) for a
single chair, at the lowest sustainable spend. This is hyper-local: the whole
addressable market is one small city + ~20 km around it.

## 0. Strategic frame (read once)

- **The funnel ends in WhatsApp.** Every ad and post drives to the WhatsApp
  deep-link / booking form on the site. No booking SaaS. So "conversion" =
  a WhatsApp message or form submit, not an online sale.
- **Organic carries the brand, paid buys reach.** A local salon lives on Reels/
  TikTok of real nail work + word of mouth. Paid ads amplify the best organic
  pieces and run always-on local-awareness. Authenticity beats polish — phone-
  shot before/after clips outperform glossy studio ads for local services.
- **One chair = a capacity ceiling.** Don't scale spend past what one operator
  can book. Target is "calendar comfortably full," not "max leads." Over-
  advertising a single-chair salon wastes money and creates no-shows.
- **Romania / Eastern EU is cheap reach.** Meta CPM in RO is roughly $2.5–5.5
  (lower than Western EU); Instagram runs ~20–40% above Facebook. A small daily
  budget goes far in one city.

## 1. Foundations (do before spending a leu)

- [ ] **Instagram → Business account**, linked to a **Facebook Page** (required
  for ads + for the comment/DM bots later). Same Meta Business Suite.
- [ ] **TikTok Business account.**
- [ ] **Meta Pixel / Conversions API** on the static site — fire a `Contact`
  /`Lead` event when the WhatsApp button or Formspree form is used. (Needs a
  cookie-consent gate; see `../../corpus/LEGAL.md` — keep it consent-aware.)
- [ ] **Google Business Profile** created & verified (free; huge for "salon
  unghii Târgu-Jiu" local search + Maps). Not Meta, but highest local ROI.
- [ ] Real **contact data live** on the site (replace placeholders) so ads have
  somewhere real to send people.
- [ ] **UTM links** for every channel so we can see what books.
- [ ] Decide tracked **conversion event** = WhatsApp click + form submit.

## 2. Organic content engine (the unfair advantage, ~free)

- [ ] **Cadence: 3–5 posts/week.** Reels/TikTok first (organic reach in 2026 is
  video, not static). Repurpose the same clip to IG Reels + TikTok + FB.
- [ ] **Content pillars** (rotate):
  - *Transformări* — before/after, the satisfying reveal (top performer).
  - *Process / ASMR* — filing, gel application, the chair experience.
  - *Nail art / design-uri* — showcase the 3rd service, trend designs.
  - *Igienă/sterilizare* — autoclav, single-use, disinfection (huge trust
    signal in RO; ties to the site's planned sterilization section).
  - *Behind-the-scenes / Ana* — face of the brand, builds the personal salon.
  - *Educational* — "cât rezistă gelul", aftercare, cum aleg forma.
- [ ] **Local hooks**: tag location (Târgu-Jiu / Gorj), use RO + local hashtags,
  collaborate with nearby non-competing businesses (hair, makeup, bridal).
- [ ] **Always a CTA + booking path**: link in bio → site; "scrie-mi pe
  WhatsApp"; comment-keyword (e.g. "PROGRAM") that the IG bot turns into a DM.
- [ ] **Stories daily**: slots libere săptămâna asta, polls, countdowns to promos.
- [ ] **Collect UGC + reviews**: ask happy clients to tag the salon; reshare.

## 3. Paid campaigns (Meta) — start small, local, always-on

Budget philosophy: **consolidate, don't fragment.** One well-fed campaign beats
five starved ones. For a single city + tiny budget, prefer tight geo + simple
structure over many ad sets.

- [ ] **Geo**: radius around the salon (start ~10–15 km), RO language, age ~18–55,
  women-leaning but let Advantage+ Audience expand (only location + min age are
  hard constraints; everything else is a soft signal in 2026).
- [ ] **Suggested starter budget**: ~30–50 RON/day (~€6–10). This is below the
  "Advantage+ optimal" thresholds quoted for ecommerce, which is fine — we're
  hyper-local with a tiny audience and a low-cost market, and we optimize for
  cheap *engagement/contact*, not 50 purchases/week. Scale only if the calendar
  has room. Raise budget ≤20% every 3–4 days to avoid resetting learning.
- [ ] **Campaign A — Local awareness / engagement (always-on):**
  - Objective: Engagement or Reach, boosting the **best organic Reel** each week.
  - Cheap top-of-funnel; keeps Ana visible in town. ~50–60% of budget.
- [ ] **Campaign B — Leads / Messages (the booker):**
  - Objective: Messages (Click-to-WhatsApp) or Leads.
  - Sends straight into a WhatsApp thread → handed to the WhatsApp bot for
    instant reply + booking. ~40–50% of budget.
  - Click-to-WhatsApp ads are the single best fit for this business.
- [ ] **Creative**: 3–4 variations, refreshed every 2–4 weeks. Real footage,
  vertical 9:16, RO captions, first 2 seconds = the transformation/hook.
  Each ad names the offer + "programează-te pe WhatsApp".
- [ ] **Offers to test**: first-visit discount, "design-ul lunii", loyalty card
  (ties to the site's loyalty section), gift voucher (seasonal).

## 4. Paid campaigns (TikTok) — optional, test later

- [ ] Start ~$5–10/day (~25–50 RON) on Spark Ads boosting an organic clip that
  already did well. Same vertical creative as Meta. Only after organic TikTok
  shows traction; for many small RO local salons Meta + organic is enough.

## 5. Seasonal calendar (RO)

- [ ] **8 Martie**, **Paște**, **nunți (mai–sept)**, **Crăciun/Revelion**,
  **Dragobete/Valentine's** — peak nail demand. Pre-plan promos + raise budget
  ~2–4 weeks ahead. Note: Meta CPMs spike ~30–60% in Oct–Dec (Q4) — budget for it.
- [ ] Tie promos to the loyalty/voucher section already on the site.

## 6. Measurement & cadence

- [ ] **Weekly 15-min review**: spend, cost per WhatsApp lead, bookings, no-show
  rate, which creative/offer won. Kill losers, scale winners' budget.
- [ ] **North-star metric**: *cost per booked appointment*, not CPM or likes.
- [ ] **Capacity guardrail**: if Ana is fully booked 1+ week out, pause the
  Messages campaign and let organic + awareness coast.
- [ ] Reconcile UTM/lead source so we know paid vs organic vs Google Profile.

## 7. What we deliberately do NOT do

- ❌ Buy followers / likes / engagement (kills reach, looks fake, against ToS).
- ❌ Run ads to a generic "boost post" with no offer or CTA.
- ❌ Scale spend faster than one chair can serve.
- ❌ Target outside the realistic catchment area.
